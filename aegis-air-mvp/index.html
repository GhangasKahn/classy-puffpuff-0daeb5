Important limitations
  • This is not a medical device and does not diagnose illness.
  • NASA EONET is a curated event catalog, not a complete satellite hotspot feed.

  • Open-Meteo/CAMS values are modeled data, not EPA AirNow regulatory observations.
  • SpO₂ is recorded but deliberately not interpreted or scored. Heart rate is used only as a relative deviation from your entered baseline.
