Interactive breakdown of the Prairie removable fence — explode the model, walk assembly, check off lumber, study joinery, open blueprints, and keep site drafts on-device.

How to use this app

  1. Open Visualize — explode the fence and inspect parts
  2. Work Assembly checklist phase by phase
  3. Shop from Materials (≈303 bf)
  4. Save drop-off / gray / latch choice in Drafts
  5. Print or download CAD & blueprint Files
